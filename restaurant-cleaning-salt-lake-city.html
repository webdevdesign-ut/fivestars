Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be on site?

No—as long as there's a way for us to get in. Just ensure utilities are on and the place is empty. We can text photo updates and lock up when finished.

Do you handle health-code checklists?

We align to your checklist and provide QA notes.

Do you provide supplies?

Yes, we provide all standard cleaning supplies. We can also stock restroom consumables upon request.

Are your cleaning products food-safe?

Yes, we only use eco-friendly and food-safe cleaning products approved for use in kitchens and dining areas.

Can we add window or periodic deep cleans?

Yes—bundle glass and periodic FOH/BOH deep cleans.

Do you provide certifications for hood and vent cleaning?

Yes, we can provide proof of service and certification for hood and vent cleaning, ensuring compliance with local regulations.

Can you clean after-hours?

Absolutely. We offer flexible after-hours and overnight cleaning so your restaurant stays spotless without disrupting service. Nightly and early-morning service available.

Are your cleaners insured and trained?

Absolutely. Every member of our cleaning team is fully insured, bonded, trained, and background-checked for your peace of mind.

Pricing depends on size, frequency, and specific needs. Contact us for a custom quote.
